# Simple Terms Guide for World Cup Quiz Questions

This guide helps you understand simplified terms used in questions.

## Common Simplified Terms:

**Instead of:** "Groin muscle tear" → **Use:** "Leg muscle tear"
**Instead of:** "Layoff pass" → **Use:** "Short pass"
**Instead of:** "Dummy run" → **Use:** "Fake run"
**Instead of:** "Through ball" → **Use:** "Long pass"
**Instead of:** "Semi-final" → **Use:** "Semi-final" (keep as is)
**Instead of:** "Group stage" → **Use:** "First round"
**Instead of:** "Hat-trick" → **Use:** "3 goals" or "scored 3 goals"
**Instead of:** "Playmaker" → **Use:** "Creative player"
**Instead of:** "Iberian nation" → **Use:** "Next to Spain" or "Portugal/Spain"
**Instead of:** "Scandinavian country" → **Use:** "Cold country in Europe" or "Northern Europe"
**Instead of:** "Participate in" → **Use:** "Play in"
**Instead of:** "Emerged as a star" → **Use:** "Became famous"
**Instead of:** "Forced out with injury" → **Use:** "Get hurt and miss games"
**Instead of:** "Fail to get past" → **Use:** "Get beaten in" or "Lose in"
**Instead of:** "Assists" → **Use:** "Helped create goals" or "Goal assists"

## Question Simplification Examples:

**Before:** "In which World Cup was Pelé forced out with injury after just 2 games?"
**After:** "In which World Cup did Pelé get hurt and miss most games?"

**Before:** "How many assists did Pelé provide in World Cups?"
**After:** "How many goals did Pelé help create (assists) in World Cups?"

**Before:** "Against which country did Pelé score a hat-trick in the 1958 World Cup semi-final?"
**After:** "Against which country did Pelé score 3 goals in the 1958 World Cup semi-final?"

## Tips for Finding Simple Terms:

1. Replace technical football terms with everyday language
2. Use "scored X goals" instead of "hat-trick"
3. Use "first round" instead of "group stage"
4. Use "get hurt" instead of "forced out with injury"
5. Use "became famous" instead of "emerged as a star"
6. Use "play in" instead of "participate in"
7. Use "help create goals" instead of just "assists"
